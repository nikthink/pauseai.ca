Montreal

We are a community of volunteers that aims to mitigate the risks of AI by convincing governments to pause the development of superhuman AI.
PauseAI inform the public, talk to policy-makers, and organize events.

At pauseai montreal, we:

  • Find people alredy concerned about ai at meetups around montreal
  • Meet and discuss how ai should be regulated, deployed, to make it safe and help humanity rather than cause issues
  • Put up posters around the city
  • Email and talk to local politians to push them to vote for ai regulation
  • ... and more to come!

If you want to get involved or have questions, join one of our meetups,
